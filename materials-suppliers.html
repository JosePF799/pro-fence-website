Built With Better Inputs

Material choice affects durability, security, appearance, and how well the finished fence holds up over time.

Why We Show This

Supplier transparency helps customers understand the quality behind the quote.

Fence materials are not all the same. Chain link gauge, post strength, gate frames, lumber quality, coating finish, and access hardware can all change how a project performs after installation.

Pro Fence sources materials based on the property, exposure, security needs, appearance, and budget, then recommends the right fit instead of treating every job like a basic fence run.

Material Priorities

  • Durable posts, rails, fittings, and fence fabric
  • Gate frames and hardware suited to daily use
  • Wood and metal products that fit the property
  • Powder coating and finish options for cleaner presentation
  • Suppliers selected according to the job scope and availability
